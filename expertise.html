Skills & Expertise

Comprehensive legal expertise across multiple domains with a focus on legislative excellence, policy formulation, and community-centered legal solutions.

Professional Skills

Legislative Drafting & Policy Formulation

Expertise in formulating and interpreting regulations and policies, coordinating the drafting of Private Members' Bills, and amending Bills to ensure legal compliance and alignment with constitutional requirements.

Legal Interpretation & Advisory

Providing legal interpretations of Acts, Bills, and legislative matters, offering comprehensive legal advice on emerging legal issues such as governance, policy, commercial, and regulatory issues.

Compliance & Constitutional Oversight

Ensuring that all Bills passed comply with the Constitution and relevant statutes, ensuring constitutional integrity in the legislative process.

Legal Representation & Court Proceedings

Representing the County Assembly and the County Assembly Service Board (CASB) in court, handling legal disputes and ensuring proper legal representation in judicial matters.

Intellectual Property & Commercial Law

Drafting legal opinions on intellectual property rights, representing clients in IP matters, and advising on the legal and regulatory requirements for commercial contracts and business compliance.

Legal Research & Document Review

Conducting legal research to stay informed of evolving laws and reviewing company constitutions, legislative documents, and commercial contracts to ensure legal compliance and alignment with new regulations.

Stakeholder Collaboration & Team Leadership

Collaborating effectively with various stakeholders, including government entities and external organizations, while leading the legal team to ensure the successful execution of legal tasks and reforms.
