Biography

I am an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya and a Commissioner for Oaths, committed to advancing legal reforms that positively impact society. I hold a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree from the University of Nairobi and focus on justice, policy development, and legislative drafting.

I serve as the Head of the Legal Department at the County Assembly of Turkana, where I support the enactment of over 20 laws and policies, including legislation on gender equality, climate change, and community development, while strengthening legislative oversight and ensuring compliance with national and international legal standards.

My role involves leading the review of the County Finance Bill and conducting legal reviews and audits of laws enacted since the advent of devolution, ensuring that the legal and policy framework remains responsive to community needs and aligned with constitutional principles.

In addition to my legislative role, I provide legal services in Commercial Law and Litigation and collaborate with key legal and policy institutions to promote inclusive, practical, and sustainable legal frameworks, particularly for marginalized communities.

Vision Statement

To be a transformative leader in the legal field, championing the development of inclusive, sustainable, and community-centered policies and legislation that drive meaningful change and empower marginalized communities across Kenya and the world in general.

Mission Statement

My mission is to leverage my expertise as an advocate and legal professional to strengthen legislative frameworks, promote justice, and ensure that laws serve the needs of vulnerable groups. I am committed to advancing the principles of equity, transparency, and accountability through innovative legal reforms and collaboration with various stakeholders, including government entities, civil society organizations, and local communities.

Career Goals

Enhance Legal Reforms

To continue strengthening and enhancing the legislative framework in Kenya, particularly in areas such as gender equality, climate change, and community-driven policies, ensuring that these reforms remain relevant and impactful.

Leadership Development

To further develop my leadership skills and influence in Turkana and beyond, building a high-performing legal team that supports the County in achieving its legislative and oversight goals.

Advocacy for Vulnerable Groups

To advocate for the development of more laws targeting vulnerable groups such as women, children, the elderly, and persons with disabilities, ensuring they have a stronger voice in policy formulation and legislative processes.

Strengthen Devolution

To contribute to the full realization of devolution in Kenya by providing legal advice and support that enables county governments to effectively manage their roles and responsibilities, especially in regions like Turkana.

Influence National Reforms

To work alongside national policymakers, think tanks, and other legal professionals to influence and contribute to Kenya's broader legal reform agenda, particularly in emerging issues such as climate change, human rights, and sustainable development.

Professional Excellence

To continuously improve my legal expertise and maintain the highest standards of professional practice, ensuring that I provide the best possible service to my clients and the communities I serve.
